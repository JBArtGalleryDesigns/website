About The Artist

My name is Jibreel. I am a visually impaired artist who draws animal print designs. I can design custom made animal print hats and shirts. I can design Giraffe prints, Thunder Bird prints, Eagle prints, and Butterfly prints. All designs can be bought printed on to T-shirts, hats, and tank tops. I can also create Art Portraits with those same animal prints.
